What Is Good Work?

Good work is the full and faithful exercise of your role — whatever that role is — in a way that serves not only your immediate purpose but the people, systems, and world your work touches. It applies to every individual in their daily decisions, every company in how it builds and operates, and every community or group in how it organizes itself and treats those within and beyond its borders.

It is work done with responsibility, equity, sustainability, and transparency as operating standards, not aspirations. It is not perfect work. It is not easy work. It is work that asks, consistently and honestly: who does this affect, is it fair, will it last, and am I telling the truth about it?

And when each one of us — in our unique role — commits to that standard, something larger than any single act begins to happen. The effects compound. The culture shifts. The ripple moves outward in ways that cannot always be measured but can always be felt. That is not idealism. That is how change has always actually worked.

The Core Belief
"Every person and every entity, in their unique role, has both the power and the responsibility to do good — for every individual they touch, every organization they serve, and every community they are part of. Good work and great success are not in conflict. They are the same thing."
The Philosophy

Why good work
changes
everything.

GOOD WORK TOGETHERSM is built on a belief that is both simple and radical: that doing good and doing well are not in conflict — they are, in fact, the same thing. Every system has an underlying business — a company, a government, a religious institution, an entertainment enterprise, a family, even an individual's own personal organization — and R.E.S.T.SM is how that business is governed, focused, and improved.

The philosophy begins with the individual. Every person, in every role — executive or employee, founder or first-year, in any industry, at any level — has the power to do good for everyone their work touches. That is not a small thing. It is everything. Because when individuals do good work, it compounds. Teams shift. Organizations change. Industries transform. And the wave moves outward.

This is not idealism. This is not a rejection of ambition, profit, or success. In fact, the greater you become, the more good you can do. The belief at the heart of GOOD WORK TOGETHERSM is that success and goodness are the same transaction — that building something exceptional and doing right by the people your work touches are not competing goals. They are one goal, pursued together.

"The false choice between doing good and doing well has cost organizations — and the world — more than we can calculate. It is time to retire it."

GOOD WORK TOGETHERSM exists to make this belief operational — through frameworks, education, advisory work, and a growing community of leaders who refuse to accept that business as usual is the only option.

The Framework

R.E.S.T.SM
The structure beneath
all good work.

R.E.S.T.SM stands for Responsibility, Equity, Sustainability, and Transparency. It is an integrated operational framework — not a compliance checklist or four separate initiatives. It is how good work gets done in every role, sector, and level of an organization — and in every community and individual life.

R
Responsibility

Owning the full impact of your decisions — on people, communities, environments, and the future. Not just within your walls, but across every life your role touches.

E
Equity

Ensuring that doing good reaches everyone — not just those at the center of power. Building systems, cultures, and practices where fairness is structural, not incidental.

S
Sustainability

Building for the long term — for the environment, for communities, for organizations, and for people. Good work that lasts requires thinking beyond the next quarter.

T
Transparency

Operating with honesty and accountability in all things. Saying what you mean, meaning what you say, and being willing to show your work — to stakeholders, to the public, to history.

About Deidre

Thought Leader. Speaker.
Advisor. Consultant.
Lawyer. Executive. Founder.

Deidre Brianne Richardson has spent more than two decades in the rooms where decisions get made — boardrooms, C-suites, legal departments, and governance committees at some of the world's largest organizations. She has seen, firsthand, what happens when companies and leaders focus only on one side of the equation — their own success, their own metrics, their own survival — and ignore the people, communities, and living things their decisions touch.

She has also seen the opposite. And it changes everything.

GOOD WORK TOGETHERSM exists because of that difference. It is built on a philosophy as simple as it is transformative — that every individual, every company, and every community has both the power and the responsibility to do good for everyone and everything they touch.

Deidre brings to this work the full weight of her experience — as a lawyer who understands systems and accountability, as a C-suite executive who has built programs that actually work, as an entrepreneur who has bet her professional life on this belief, and as a human being who refuses to accept that the world cannot be better than it is.

She holds a J.D. and an MBA, and has served as General Counsel, Chief Legal Officer, Chief Compliance Officer, and Corporate Secretary across industries including services, food & beverage, fashion, entertainment, media & marketing, consumer products, and global operations spanning more than 150 countries and 18,000+ employees. Today, she works as an advisor and consultant, bringing that same executive-level governance experience to organizations and leaders applying the R.E.S.T.SM framework.
